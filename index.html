FAQ

How does this work?

We deliver for our customers from anywhere our customers would like. We take your order by internet and place it with the "mother bet". We dispatch a meal courier to pick up the food, pack it in an insulated carrier and deliver it directly to you. Usually, your meal courier will be waiting at the restaurant when the food comes out of the kitchen.

How long does it take for a delivery?

Our normal delivery time is between 30 minutes and 45 minutes, however certain situations such as traffic, weather, and food preparation time require extra time. Please know that we are always working hard to get your food delivered as quickly as possible. We appreciate your patience. Placing orders in advance is appreciated.

Who do I call if there is a mistake with my order?

Please call our delivery service +251 900 01 02 03 within thirty minutes of delivery if there is anything missing or incorrect. We will get incorrect food items replaced.

How do I cancel my order?

If you need to cancel your order please contact us at +251 900 01 02 03. Please keep in mind that we strive to process and deliver orders on time, and once you recieved your order we will be subject to our return policy.

How do I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team via: Email: info@MBD.com Phone: +251 900 01 02 03 Telegram: MotherBetDelivery
